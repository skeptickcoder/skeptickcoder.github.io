The engine

Under the hood: read the source, cache like you mean it.

Careers pages are frontends over applicant-tracking systems with public JSON APIs. JobFinder speaks to those APIs directly — 7 adapters, 53 companies, ~4,300 postings a scan — and revalidates with ETags so unchanged boards cost zero bytes.
